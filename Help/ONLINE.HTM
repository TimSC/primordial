Registering Online

 
Registering online is typically the fastest method of registration.

You can register Primordial Life online though Kagi payment services. Kagi collects payments for hundreds of shareware authors all over the world. To learn more about Kagi, see their website at www.kagi.com.

Make sure you are connected to the Internet before you attempt to register online.

Register Online for browsers that support SSL (Secure Socket Layer) like recent versions of Internet Explorer and Netscape Navigator.
